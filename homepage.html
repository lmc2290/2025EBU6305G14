About Our Project

Empowering Digital Media Education

Our website is designed to help users learn about digital media concepts, particularly image compression and video encoding, through interactive lessons and engaging games.

We aim to make learning fun and accessible for students, educators, and anyone interested in digital media. Our team at Queen Mary University of London has developed this platform to enhance the educational experience.
